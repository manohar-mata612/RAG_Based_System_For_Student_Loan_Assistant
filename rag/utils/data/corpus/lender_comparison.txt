========================================================

This document compares the three primary lenders in this corpus: Avanse Financial Services, MPOWER Financing, and Prodigy Finance. It is intended to help the AI assistant answer comparison questions accurately.

=======================================================
COLLATERAL REQUIREMENT
=======================================================
Avanse:       NO collateral required for many loan amounts. Collateral may be required for very high loan amounts or specific profiles. When collateral is provided, it can enable higher amounts or different pricing.
MPOWER:       NO collateral required. Ever. This is a core feature of the MPOWER product.
Prodigy Finance: NO collateral required (standard product). This is a core feature of the standard Prodigy Finance product.

=======================================================
COSIGNER REQUIREMENT
=======================================================
Avanse:       YES — a co-borrower (parent, legal guardian, sibling, or other blood relative) is required. The co-borrower is the primary debtor.
MPOWER:       NO cosigner required. MPOWER is specifically designed to give loans without requiring a U.S. cosigner or collateral.
Prodigy Finance: NO cosigner required (standard product). Optional co-signer product available only for eligible Indian students.

=======================================================
INTEREST RATE TYPE
=======================================================
Avanse:       Floating (variable) rate. Formula: Avanse Base Rate + Spread. Current Base Rate: 14.55% (WEF 01.12.2024). Student-friendly rate starts at 9.99%.
MPOWER:       Fixed rate. As low as 9.99% (10.89% APR). Rate includes 0.25% discount for auto-pay enrollment.
Prodigy Finance: Variable rate. Starting from 9.97%. Representative APR: 12.15% variable. Made up of fixed margin + benchmark rate (e.g., SOFR).

=======================================================
LOAN AMOUNTS
=======================================================
Avanse:       Up to Rs. 1.25 Crores (approximately USD 150,000). Loan in Indian Rupees for Indian students.
MPOWER:       Minimum US$2,001 per academic period. Maximum US$50,000 per academic period. Lifetime limit: US$100,000.
Prodigy Finance: Aligned to school-certified Cost of Attendance (CoA). No specific cap stated beyond CoA; representative example: USD 40,000 borrowed.

=======================================================
ELIGIBLE BORROWERS
=======================================================
Avanse:       Indian citizens aged 18+, studying in India or abroad, with confirmed admission to an approved institution.
MPOWER:       International students from 150+ countries of citizenship, attending 500+ eligible U.S. and Canadian schools. No U.S. credit history or cosigner needed.
Prodigy Finance: International postgraduate (master's level) students from 120+ countries, studying at 1,500+ supported schools worldwide.

=======================================================
STUDY DESTINATION
=======================================================
Avanse:       India and abroad (USA, UK, Australia, Canada, France, Germany, New Zealand, Singapore, UAE, others)
MPOWER:       U.S. or Canada only (must attend an MPOWER-eligible school)
Prodigy Finance: Worldwide (U.S., UK, Germany, France, Canada, Australia, and others at 1,500+ schools)

=======================================================
LOAN LEVEL (UNDERGRADUATE / GRADUATE)
=======================================================
Avanse:       Graduate, post-graduate, or job-oriented professional/technical courses. Also undergraduate.
MPOWER:       Both undergraduate and graduate students at eligible schools (junior, senior year or graduate students for U.S. schools; must be within 2 years of graduating)
Prodigy Finance: Primarily postgraduate (master's level) students

=======================================================
REPAYMENT / GRACE PERIOD
=======================================================
Avanse:       Moratorium = course duration + 3 to 6 months. Can start paying interest during the course to reduce later EMIs.
MPOWER:       Interest-only payments during school AND for 6 months after graduation. Full repayment begins after the 6-month grace period. Repayment term: approximately 10 years.
Prodigy Finance: Reduced payment (USD 100/month) for first 30 months (in-school + initial post-grad period). Full repayment (USD 573.36/month example) for remaining 150 months. Total: 180 months (15 years).

=======================================================
PREPAYMENT PENALTY
=======================================================
Avanse:       Not explicitly stated; check sanction letter
MPOWER:       NO prepayment penalty — explicitly stated
Prodigy Finance: NO prepayment penalty — explicitly stated

=======================================================
PROCESSING TIME / SANCTION SPEED
=======================================================
Avanse:       As fast as 3 days for loan sanction
MPOWER:       Conditional loan offer potentially within days; check eligibility in 30 seconds online
Prodigy Finance: Non-binding provisional offer valid for 14 days; final confirmation requires document verification and matching

=======================================================
PROCESSING FEES
=======================================================
Avanse:       One-time processing fee (small percentage of sanction amount or flat fee; detailed in sanction letter)
MPOWER:       No explicit processing fee mentioned (rates include the 0.25% auto-pay discount in the stated APR)
Prodigy Finance: USD 500 processing fee (paid after accepting the offer; only upfront fee required)

=======================================================
VISA SUPPORT LETTER
=======================================================
Avanse:       Provides loan documentation for university admission and visa processes
MPOWER:       Provides FREE visa support letters as part of its services; loan acceptance letters can be used as proof of funds
Prodigy Finance: Loan offer letters can be used as proof of financial readiness for visa applications

=======================================================
PROOF OF FUNDS ACCEPTED?
=======================================================
Avanse:       Yes — sanction letter and documentation provided for visa and enrollment
MPOWER:       Yes — MPOWER explicitly states loan acceptance letters are accepted by many universities and immigration authorities as valid proof of funds
Prodigy Finance: Yes — Prodigy Finance loan offer letters can be used as proof of financial readiness

=======================================================
CREDIT BUILDING
=======================================================
Avanse:       Timely repayment builds credit history in India. Delay or default negatively impacts credit score of student and co-applicants.
MPOWER:       Builds U.S. credit history through on-time payments — explicitly a core benefit
Prodigy Finance: Not explicitly mentioned in scraped content

=======================================================
CPT / OPT IMPACT ON LOAN ELIGIBILITY
=======================================================
Avanse:       CPT/OPT are U.S.-specific work authorizations. Avanse is an Indian lender; CPT/OPT status does not directly affect loan eligibility with Avanse, but income from CPT/OPT can help repayment.
MPOWER:       MPOWER explicitly acknowledges that international F-1 students can work up to 20 hours on campus and that OPT/CPT are available for off-campus work. MPOWER models future earning potential (including likely OPT employment). CPT/OPT status does not make a student ineligible for an MPOWER loan.
Prodigy Finance: Prodigy Finance models future earning potential. CPT/OPT work authorization does not make a student ineligible for a Prodigy Finance loan. Students working on OPT post-graduation have income to support repayment.

=======================================================
REFINANCING AVAILABLE?
=======================================================
Avanse:       Yes — top-up after fresh assessment possible
MPOWER:       Yes — MPOWER offers refinancing for international students without requiring a cosigner
Prodigy Finance: Not explicitly detailed in scraped content

=======================================================
FEDERAL LOANS ALTERNATIVE
=======================================================
IMPORTANT: International students (non-U.S. citizens who are not eligible noncitizens) are NOT eligible for U.S. federal student loans. They are also NOT eligible for:
- Federal income-driven repayment plans (IBR, PAYE, SAVE, ICR)
- Public Service Loan Forgiveness (PSLF)
- Any other federal student aid programs

Private lenders (Avanse, MPOWER, Prodigy Finance) are the primary options for international students.
